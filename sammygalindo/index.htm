Welcome to the website of Aesthetics Specialist Sammy Galindo.

Sammy Galindo was born and raised in Dallas, Texas. After periods in Los Angeles and Seattle Sammy decided to relocate to the UK. Sammy is now resident in London.

Sammy has been doing hair and makeup for over ten years. He began his career working for Toni and Guy as a chemical technician as well as teaching students from the whole southwest region of the U.S.A. Sammy also worked for M.A.C. cosmetics for many years. It was during this time he was sponsored by M.A.C. to become the personal stylist of the group "Shiny Toy Guns". After spending a number of years on the road with the band Sammy became an in demand touring stylist, working with Kill Hannah, The Sounds, Clear Static and DJ Keoki among others.

Sammy specialises in "print and film" hair and makeup. He has completed numerous fashion shows for Versace, Dolce and Gabanna, Hugo Boss, along with being a model in them. Sammy has a lot of experience in shooting fetish, pin-up and alternative scene pictures as well. Sammy considers The human form beautiful and loves working with it. Sammy is also a Burlesque and Vaudeville performer. He has performed all over the UK as well as in Ireland, Canada and the United States.
